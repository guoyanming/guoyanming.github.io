    • Biography

      Yanming Guo is currently a full professor at National Key Laboratory of Big Data and Decision, National University of Defense Technology.He got his PhD at Leiden Institute of Advanced Computer Science (LIACS), Leiden University. He has published 110+ academic papers (60+ CCF-A/B、JCR Q1), with 6000+ google scholar citations. He was selected as World's Top 2% Scientists (2022,2024,2025), the excellent young Scientist fund of Hunan Province (2025), the Outstanding Young Scholar of Changsha (2022), Outstanding Young Scholar of NUDT (2020),and won CICC Technological award second prize (rank 1st, 2025).

      His research interests lie in Computer Vision and Deep Learning, including Trustworthy AI (Adversarial Attack, Deepfake, Jailbreaking, and etc), Multimedia information analysis (Cross-modal Retrieval, Visual Question Answering), UAV Remote Sensing, Large Language Model, etc.

      Collaborations: I am always seeking highly self-motivated undergraduate, graduate, PhD candidates and postdoctoral researchers for research collaborations in the fields of Computer Vision and Deep Learning. Please feel free to contact me through guoyanming[at]{nudt.edu.cn}.

      News: My book 《Deep Learning and Computer Vision Applications（深度学习及计算机视觉应用）》 was published in January 2016, based on my deep learning course at NUDT.
